About This Book

In today's interconnected world, secure and efficient networking solutions are essential for enterprises of all sizes. Traditional VPN solutions often struggle with scalability, management complexity, and performance limitations. Peer-to-peer VPN technologies have emerged as a compelling alternative.

This book provides a comprehensive guide to building an enterprise-grade peer-to-peer VPN Software as a Service (SaaS) from scratch. Drawing on the architectures and implementations of leading solutions like Tailscale, Netbird, and OmniEdge, we explore both the technical foundations and business considerations necessary for success.

What You'll Learn

  • P2P Network Architecture: Understand the fundamentals of peer-to-peer networking and how it applies to enterprise VPN solutions.
  • NAT Traversal Techniques: Master STUN, TURN, and ICE protocols for establishing connections across firewalls and NAT devices.
  • WireGuard Integration: Build on the WireGuard protocol for fast, secure, and modern VPN connectivity.
  • Multi-tenant SaaS Design: Architect your platform to serve multiple enterprise customers securely and efficiently.
  • Control Plane Development: Build the orchestration layer that manages your distributed VPN network.
  • Enterprise Security Patterns: Implement authentication, authorization, and audit logging for enterprise compliance.

Who Should Read This

Whether you're a software engineer looking to understand modern VPN technologies, a network administrator seeking insights into peer-to-peer networking, or an entrepreneur considering entering the VPN SaaS market, this book offers valuable knowledge and practical guidance.

About the Author

Written by OmniEdge engineers, this practical guide distills real-world experience from deploying enterprise VPN services in highly regulated environments. The team has built and operated OmniEdge, an open-source P2P VPN platform used by organizations worldwide.
