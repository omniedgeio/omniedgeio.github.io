Preface

In today's interconnected world, secure and efficient networking solutions are essential for enterprises of all sizes. Traditional VPN solutions, while effective, often struggle with scalability, management complexity, and performance limitations. Peer-to-peer VPN technologies have emerged as a compelling alternative, offering improved performance, simplified management, and enhanced scalability.

This book provides a comprehensive guide to building an enterprise-grade peer-to-peer VPN Software as a Service (SaaS) from scratch. Drawing on the architectures and implementations of leading solutions like Tailscale, Netbird, and OmniEdge, we explore both the technical foundations and business considerations necessary for success in this space.

Written by OmniEdge engineers, this practical guide distills real-world experience from deploying enterprise VPN services in highly regulated environments. If you're building your own secure networking SaaS—or just want to understand how it's done—this book is for you.

Whether you're a software engineer looking to understand the intricacies of modern VPN technologies, a network administrator seeking insights into peer-to-peer networking, or an entrepreneur considering entering the VPN SaaS market, this book offers valuable knowledge and practical guidance.
